Exercise 3.7

Recurrence:

\(X_n=n,\text{ for } 0\le n< m \\ X_n=X_{n-m}+1,\text{ for } n\ge m\)

Closed Form Hypothesis:

\(X_n=\lfloor n/m\rfloor+n\text{ mod } m \\ X_n=\lfloor n/m \rfloor+n-m\lfloor n/m\rfloor \\ X_n=n-(m-1)\lfloor n/m\rfloor\)

Inductive Proof:

\(X_0=0-(m-1)\lfloor 0/m\rfloor=0 \\ \lfloor n/m\rfloor=0,\text{ for } 0\le n< m \\ X_n=0+n-m(0)=n,\text{ for } 0\le n< m \\ X_n=n-m-(m-1)\lfloor(n-m)/m\rfloor+1,\text{ for } n\ge m \\ X_n=n-m-(m-1)\lfloor n/m\rfloor+(m-1)+1,\text{ for } n\ge m \\ X_n=n-(m-1)\lfloor n/m\rfloor,\text{ for } n\ge m \)
