Exercise 1.2

Recurrent relation:

\(A(0)=0 \\ A(n)=3A(n-1)+2, \text{for }n\ge0\)

Closed form hypothesis:

\(A(n)=3^n-1\)

Inductive Proof:

\(A(0)=3^0-1=0 \\ A(n)=3(3^{n-1}-1)+2=3^n-1\)

Exercise 1.11a

Recurrent relation:

\(D(0)=0 \\ D(2n)=2D(2n-2)+2, \text{for }n\ge0\)

Closed form hypothesis:

\(D(2n)=2^{n+1}-2\)

Inductive Proof:

\(D(2(0))=2^{0+1}-2=0 \\ s = 2n-2 \\ D(s) = 2^{(s/2)+1}-2 \\ D(2n)=2D(s)+2=2^{(s/2)+2}-4+2=2^{n+1}-2\)
